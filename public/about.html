About InterviewAI

We're on a mission to democratize interview preparation and help professionals around the world land their dream jobs through AI-powered practice sessions.

Our Story

InterviewAI was born from a simple observation: traditional interview preparation methods weren't keeping pace with the rapidly evolving job market. Talented individuals were missing out on incredible opportunities simply because they lacked access to effective practice and feedback.

Our founders, having experienced the challenges of interview preparation firsthand, envisioned a solution that would be personalized, accessible, and truly effective. They combined cutting-edge artificial intelligence with proven interview techniques to create a platform that could provide realistic practice sessions and detailed feedback to anyone, anywhere.

Today, InterviewAI serves thousands of professionals across various industries, helping them build confidence, improve their communication skills, and achieve their career goals. We're proud to be part of their success stories and remain committed to continuously improving our platform based on user feedback and technological advancements.
